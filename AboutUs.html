Our Story

DSC NUS will be a first-of-its kind. We will bring together people from diverse backgrounds, majors, years of study, genders and races.

Our common denominator? A shared purpose and drive to help local communities thrive.

We strive to bring technology to the people who have limited access, and this is only made possible with the most amazing and passionate bunch.
